Amp controls

What the controls do

Resonance is implemented as a presence-style peaking filter centered around the upper guitar-cab range. Positive values add bite and forwardness; negative values smooth the IR. The Room knob adds tiny early reflections to make a close-mic IR feel less dry.

The amp tester is a lightweight Web Audio preamp: input gain, tone filters, soft clipping, presence shaping, and the currently mixed cabinet IR in a convolver. Everything runs locally in the browser.
