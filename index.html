What the controls do

Resonance is implemented as a presence-style peaking filter centered around the upper guitar-cab range. Positive values add bite and forwardness; negative values smooth the IR. The Room knob adds tiny early reflections to make a close-mic IR feel less dry. For a totally raw blend, set Room and Resonance to zero.

This app runs fully in the browser with the Web Audio API. Your IR files are decoded locally and are not uploaded anywhere.
