HiChord

A touch-first chord instrument for iPhone. Rotate sideways, tap a chord pad, then move the joystick to reshape it.

Add HiChord to your iPhone

  1. Open this page in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Open HiChord from the new icon.
▯

Rotate your iPhone

HiChord is designed to play in landscape.
